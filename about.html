About Us

We are one of the most renowned specialists in electrical connections and micro-service tasks in Kenya

Ground Kings Electricals began as a specialist in electrical connections and micro-service tasks. Over time, we expanded our expertise to meet the growing needs of clients by becoming a comprehensive construction and finishing solutions provider.

Our team of skilled professionals is committed to ensuring safety, precision, and customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

Today, we deliver end-to-end building services — from structural works and installations to final finishing and interior design.

Our commitment is to make the construction journey simpler, faster, and stress-free for homeowners, developers, and businesses.
